🤝 What is Trusted Partner?
Trusted Partner is a special title given to a select few customers who believed in Joshua's Express from the very beginning — back when this business was just getting started.

These are the people who showed up early, placed their trust in the service, and helped make JED what it is today. This plate is our way of saying thank you.
🎁 Your Exclusive Perk
🆓
Free Service Tax — Forever
When JED's service tax is introduced, Trusted Partners are permanently exempt. You will never be charged a service fee — no matter what. This is our lifelong commitment to you for supporting us when it mattered most.
📋 Do You Have It?
The Trusted Partner plate is assigned manually by Joshua. If you have it, you'll see the ✦ Trusted Partner ✦ badge on your Member card.

Think you deserve it? Reach out to Joshua directly on WhatsApp.
